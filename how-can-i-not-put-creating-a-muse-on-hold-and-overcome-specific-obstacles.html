    How can I not put creating a muse on hold and overcome specific obstacles?

    The Breakfaster

    My workflow goes like this:

    1. I read about how to create a muse in Tim Ferriss' 4hww.
    2. I talk to some magazines' editors and tell them I needed 3 magazines being published each one year different.
    3. I never hear back from them.
    4. Sometimes I told them, the reason to do it was to compare advertisers over the years. Still no reply.

    So I put creating a muse on hold.

    1. I read that the product has to cost between 50 and 200€ or $ with a 8-10 times profit margin. Producing something for 5 to 20 € that costs 50 - 200.
    2. I didn't find products in that price range which I would buy on a regular basis. And buying it just once, how should I make a constant living if I needed new customers all the time?

    So I put creating a muse on hold.

    1. I want to create a product. This requires development.
    2. I thought about what I could do myself in the area of development.
    3. Many obstacles are to be overcome, I might have to ask a lawyer, who wants money. I might have
    4. to ask a designer, who wants money and
    5. I might have to send payment in advance for a company in china. Because it must be cheap enough to be competitive.
    6. And I don't know now, if it will sell, because the website I created for testing, was rarely visited in spite of Google adwords.
    7. The question is: is it because I used wrong keywords?
    8. Is the page not well designed?
    9. Is it this, is it that......is it that nobody wants my product?
    10. In general or because of a very property of the product?

    So I put creating a muse on hold.

    How can I not put creating a muse on hold and overcome those obstacles?
