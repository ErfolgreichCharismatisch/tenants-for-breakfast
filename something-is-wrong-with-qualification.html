    Something is wrong with qualification

    The Breakfaster

    According to pickup, shortly before I approach a woman coldly, I should be happy and smile.

    ​She is very attractive and looks up and down at me when approaching in disgust.

    ​How should I maintain a good mood then?

    ​In this moment I think: "I am doing you a favour by approaching you, girl. But it seems that you don't honour that!". Then I think "Why do I not also build up an "assholeshield", look condescendingly at her and say something even more derogatory to put her down?"

    ​So you shouldn't give her a stern look, because that's creepy?

    ​I should care about her not being creeped out? Does she? Does she care about me feeling good? Why should I?

    ​And there it goes. No conversation, nothing.

    ​But I looked more and more at couples and realized how unhappy those men were. ESPECIALLY those with children.

    ​If walking around expecting something good to happen, women just drag me down further. Like going to a friend greeting him and he says "Go fuck yourself, I will never talk to you again" hits you way more than a "Go fuck yourself" from your enemy after you put him down. If on the other hand I expect rejection and go in expecting a fight, the first attack of her like a boyfriend does not hit me, I have my shield up.

    ​How can a human being looking at you with aggression deserve your kindness because this human being has a vagina?

    ​I don't understand why you should smile and make a woman's day. For what? Putting you down? Telling you about her boyfriend? Why waste your time?

    ​But using negs made some women become resentful, others viewed it as a game and tried to up the ante. It has never led to sex.

    ​But just saying "Hey, I think you are attractive, what's your name" leads nowhere either, except right to the boyfriend line.

    ​So whatever I do, be honest or condescending-hateful, the ones I fancy never fancy me.

    ​When walking outside, all I see are angry or condescending female faces.

    ​If I used another approach and said: "Hmm, let's see if I like her" it would go like that - and always went like that in the past:

    ​*Some preceding conversation*

    ​Me: Hey, what do you do for a living?

    ​She: "I am studying Y"

    ​I don't care about Y. She failed.

    ​Me: "Okay, so what do you do when you have free time?"

    ​She: "I go to the cinema"

    ​I think "I go skydiving and all she can do is going to the cinema"

    ​My summary: a boring human being who is really attractive. But because pickup people said, she would be attracted if you liked her character and if she had to work for it, I do the stupid qualification. So I qualify her more and she fails every test.

    ​But my genitals do not care. And neither do I.

    ​"But hey, it is so important for her to work for it."

    ​So I say: "Okay, you are not good enough for me"

    ​She: "Yeah whatever"

    ​We split. Great outcome. I qualifed that women, standing ovations by imaginary pickup artists.

    ​In short, a woman will never pass my shittests. So why even bother qualifying her, because someone in the community said so? Really?

    ​They live in this fairy tale world where there are countless available and sexy women in daygame and you could qualify.

    ​Reality: most women look average or bad. Randomly there is an attractive girl. I cannot qualify her, because I want her.

    ​This pickup shit drifted so far off male interests. I do not want to make her day and pamper her with a good qualifying experience.

    ​Something is really wrong with qualification.
