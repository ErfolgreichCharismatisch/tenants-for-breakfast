    The calming power of "I don't know"

    The Breakfaster

    You talk to someone about an issue you are facing and he replies with "I don't know". Even though you did not get the desired answer, it calms you to not hear some pathetic approach to save face.

    I don't know

    You can say - if the IDK is sincere - thank you and move on to someone else. Nobody's time is wasted. Everyone wins.
