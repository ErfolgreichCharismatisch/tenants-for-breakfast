    When prospects don't want to pay the deposit

    The Breakfaster

    Recently a potential tenant said:

    You know, I cannot pay the deposit, because, you know, I don't have money as I am a student. But I assume that's fine with you.

    I replied with

    You know, I cannot have you as a tenant, because, you know, I have to take a deposit, because if you don't pay, I have to. But I assume, you understand.

    Internally I was furious. How does this little shit dare to think that because she is a student she was free to not pay a deposit. And this assumption that the "deal was already closed". But with this statement, I got a little bit of my dignity back.

    Still furious, but less.
